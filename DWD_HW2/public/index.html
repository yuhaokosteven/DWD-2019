LifeShare

Share Your Depression Story

LifeShare is an interactive application that creates a safe Augmented Reality environment that people can get help and help others in Taiwan. For people who are depressed and anxious, this is a place that people can share their experience. Besides, talking and listening to other people with same suffering stories can have peer to peer support connection. This can alleviate the feeling of loneliness. After all, sometimes talking changes everything.

Thesis Statement

I propose an interactive environment that allows young people in Taiwan to share and talk about their depression or anxiety in order to combat stigmas about mental health.
